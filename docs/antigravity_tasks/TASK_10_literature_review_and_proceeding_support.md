# TASK 10 — Literature Review and JACOW Proceeding Support

## Objective

Create project documentation to support the introduction, literature review, and figures for a JACOW-style conference proceeding.

## Required documents

- `docs/literature/literature_review.md`
- `docs/proceedings/jacow_outline.md`

## Literature topics

Cover:

1. residual-gas space-charge compensation in low-energy beam transport,
2. electron column concept,
3. electron lens as related externally supplied compensation,
4. high-current cyclotron axial injection limitations,
5. buncher effects and reduced neutralization of peak bunch charge,
6. gas species choice: H2 compatibility and Kr fast ionization response,
7. multiple scattering and gas-load constraints.

## Constraints

- Separate literature-supported claims from this project’s simulation results.
- Do not overclaim full compensation of bunched beams.
- Use JACOW-compatible LaTeX formatting.
- Keep equations compact.

## Deliverables

- Literature review markdown.
- JACOW outline.
- Introduction draft.
- Slide outline with figure list.

## Acceptance criteria

- The intro correctly states the neutralizer is before the main solenoid.
- The paper explains why previous space-charge compensation work is relevant but not identical to this single-pass cyclotron injection problem.
